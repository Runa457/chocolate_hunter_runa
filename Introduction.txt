 -Hunt for chocolates!-

Chocolate hunter Runa is addicted to chocolate.
Help her earn chocolate through battle.

 -In the camp-

She must eat enough chocolate in a certain amount of time.
She can extend her life by eating chocolate.

She can make potions by extracting ingredients from chocolate to recover her health.

She can upgrade equipments by special magic which costs large amount of chocolate.

 -In the route-

There are three paths ahead.

 -In the battle-

She can fight with two types of attack.

Melee type is one-targeted attack.

She can also cast spells for powerful attack, but it costs mana.
